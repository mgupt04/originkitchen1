About Simply Green

Our Story

Simply Green was born from a simple idea: making healthy eating both accessible and enjoyable. Founded in 2024 in the heart of the city, we started as a small smoothie bar with big dreams. Today, we've grown into a community hub for health-conscious individuals who don't want to compromise on taste.

Our journey began when our founders, passionate about healthy living and environmental sustainability, noticed a gap in the market for truly healthy fast food options. They envisioned a place where busy professionals, families, and students could grab nutritious meals that are both delicious and environmentally responsible.

Our Mission

At Simply Green, our mission is threefold:

  • To serve fresh, nutritious food that delights the palate and nourishes the body
  • To promote sustainable eating habits that benefit both our customers and the planet
  • To create a positive impact in our community through ethical business practices

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Sourcing

We partner with local organic farmers and suppliers to source the freshest ingredients while reducing our carbon footprint. All our produce is seasonal and sustainably grown.

Packaging

Our commitment to the environment extends to our packaging - all our containers and utensils are either compostable or made from recycled materials.

Zero Waste

We're working towards becoming a zero-waste establishment by composting food scraps, recycling, and implementing water conservation practices in our daily operations.

Core Values

  • Quality: Using only the finest, freshest ingredients
  • Integrity: Being transparent about our sourcing and practices
  • Community: Supporting local farmers and giving back to our neighborhood
  • Innovation: Constantly improving our menu and sustainability practices
  • Health: Prioritizing nutritious options without compromising taste
